About Me

My name is Joe Hurowitz. I've been a musician and school teacher for many years. I am currently teaching in Washington DC while attending the GW University Web Development Bootcamp. I hope one day to work for an educational organization or a non-profit who need a coder to help develop some great curriculum for the students of the world.
Here are some of the subjects we covered over the course of 24 weeks: Javascript, jQuery, Node.js, Html, CSS, mySQL, Sequelize, express server, GitHub , REACT Framework, MongoDB, and ES6.
Lastly I'd like to add that I'm the father of a wonderful 11 year old named Madeleine and I'm a creative musician and leader of a band called "The Mercy Alliance".
